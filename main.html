A bucket list is a collection of experiences or achievements a person wants to accomplish before they "kick the bucket"—a euphemism for dying. It often includes goals or dreams that are meaningful or exciting to the individual. The term comes from the idea of a list of things to do before one’s time is up. This list can include anything from traveling to specific destinations, learning new skills, or achieving personal milestones. It’s a way for people to set intentions and prioritize what they want to do with their lives.
